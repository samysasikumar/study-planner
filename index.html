02 / Data Science TSA School Competition
📊
International Tourism Arrivals — India & USA

Analyzed international tourist arrivals for India and the United States from 1995–2019 using World Bank open data. Applied linear regression in Excel to model growth trends and forecast arrivals through 2025, capturing pre-COVID baselines and post-pandemic recovery projections.

Key Finding
India grew from ~2M to ~18M arrivals (1995–2019), showing rapid growth potential. The USA maintained ~80–175M arrivals with projected recovery to ~200M by 2025 — two very different tourism profiles responding differently to the pandemic.
